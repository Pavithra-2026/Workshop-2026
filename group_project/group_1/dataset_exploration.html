This document explores all the CSV files found in the data/ folder. There are four distinct datasets, each in its own subfolder. For every file we’ll describe what a single row represents and what each column means.

Dataset A — Yogurt Study (data/group_a_yogurt/)

A study looking at how eating yogurt affects the vaginal microbiome. Participants were assigned to either a yogurt group or an unchanged diet group, and samples were taken at baseline and after antibiotic treatment.

00_sample_ids_yogurt.csv

What a row represents: one biological sample collected from one participant at one time point.

Columns:

Column Meaning
pid Participant ID (e.g. pid_25). Links to 01_participant_metadata_yogurt.csv
time_point When the sample was taken: baseline or after_antibiotic
arm Study group assignment: yogurt or unchanged_diet
sample_id Unique sample identifier (e.g. UNCH001). Links to 02_qpcr_results_yogurt.csv and 03_luminex_results_yogurt.csv

Column Meaning

limits Whether the value was within limits (reliable) or out of range (too low/high to measure accurately)

cd3_negative Immune cells that are not T-cells (CD3 is a T-cell marker)

cd4_t_cells Helper T-cells — they coordinate immune responses
cd8_t_cells Killer T-cells — they destroy infected cells

Dataset D — Sorghum Timecourse (data/modeling/01-sorghum_timecourse.csv)

A plant biology dataset tracking gene expression in sorghum (a grain crop) across a growing season. Two genotypes (BT642 and RT430) were grown under different conditions (Control, Preflowering, Postflowering), and leaf samples were collected weekly from Day 14 to Day 98.

The first nine columns are gene expression values — each is the expression level of one specific sorghum gene in that sample.

01-sorghum_timecourse.csv

What a row represents: one leaf sample collected at one time point from one plant (or pool of plants), with expression measurements for nine genes.

Columns:

Column Meaning
Sobic.001G062300.v3.1 through Sobic.003G421700.v3.1 Expression level of each sorghum gene (9 genes total). These are Sorghum bicolor gene IDs from genome version 3.1
Barcode Sequencing barcode used to identify this sample in the lab
libraryName Sequencing library name (e.g. BOCAX)
Block Field block number (e.g. Block 05)
Week Week of the study (3–14)
Replicate Biological replicate number (1–3)
Genotype Sorghum variety: BT642 or RT430
Condition Growth condition: Control, Preflowering, or Postflowering
Sample.type Type of tissue sampled (all Leaves)
Day Day of the study (Day14 through Day98)
Row Field row position
isFloweringWeek Whether this sample was collected during the week the plant flowered (TRUE/FALSE)
Date.Harvested Calendar date of harvest
Collection.Time Time of day collected
No.plants.pooled Number of plants pooled into this sample
FloweringJulianDate Julian date (day of year) when the plant flowered
FloweringDate Calendar date when the plant flowered
FloweringWeek Week number when flowering occurred
FloweringDay Day of study when flowering occurred
Unreplicated Whether this sample had no replication (TRUE/FALSE)
CompleteSampleTreatment Composite label combining Genotype, Week, and Condition (e.g. RT430:03:Preflowering)
Timepoint Numeric week (same as Week)
Group Composite label combining Genotype and Condition (e.g. RT430:Preflowering)
